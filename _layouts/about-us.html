About Claxton Handi House

Claxton Handi House has been serving the Claxton community for more than 15 years. It's owner, Joe Blocker, started the business in 2000 four miles out the community limits of Claxton, GA.

Claxton Handi house sells carports and portable storage buildings of various sizes and designs. Joe also has the pleasure of supplying steel buildings to serve as shops, hay barns, or garages.

If you've got an idea for a building on your property, call Joe today and Claxton Handi House will get right on discovering the best option to fit your needs.
